About Mazeri

Mazeri is a village in Becho community, Mestia Municipality, Samegrelo-Zemo Svaneti Region, Georgia. Located in the valley of the river Dolrachala (right tributary of the Enguri). 1660 m above sea level, 25 km from Mestia.

The trail is rich in panoramas and spectacular views. On the route you will be able to see: Gulba Peaks, Ushba Peaks, Laila, Tetnuldi, Shkhara, Gestolda, Ktin, Rustaveli Peak, Aylama and other beautiful landscapes, pastures and dense forests.

The camp is everywhere you want to camp.

Discover unique experiences in nature reserves, farms, vineyards and public camps, tree houses, cabins, car camping, small houses, camping, folding tents and more.
