About Gomismta

The route starts from Gomismti resort and goes to Bakhmaro resort. Resort Gomismata is located at 2100-2755 meters above sea level, Bakhmaro 1950-2050 meters.

The starting point of the route is 2000 meters above sea level, the final point is 1950 meters. Walking distance 40 km. The highest point is 2614 meters (wolf bed). Lifting height in total 1470 meters, and lifting height in total 1485 meters.

An off-road vehicle is required to cross the route. It is possible to walk the route, in this case the route takes 2 days.

The camp is everywhere you want to camp.

Discover unique experiences in nature reserves, farms, vineyards and public camps, tree houses, cabins, car camping, small houses, camping, folding tents and more.
